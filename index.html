Frequently Asked Questions

How should we divide our work?

+

As a team, start by identifying each member's strengths. A simple example : If someone excels in backend development, assign them backend tasks. Likewise, let the frontend expert handle frontend work. For junior members, provide guidance by giving them manageable tasks that help them learn. Additionally, designate a team leader to coordinate efforts and keep the project on track. This approach ensures that responsibilities are distributed effectively, and everyone gets a chance to contribute.

How do I select and submit my project for review?

+

Submit your project using the designated form , which will be active starting at 02:00 PM on 15th June, 2025 till 08:00 PM on 15th June 2025. Ensure you provide the following details:

  • Team Name (You will have to select your team name from the options)
  • GitHub Repository Link: Must be named after your team (case-sensitive)
  • Project Selected (You will have to select your project name from the options)
  • Presentation Video Link: You and your team member will present your project, record the presentation, and share the recording link. The video can be uploaded to either Google Drive or YouTube and must be accessible for public viewing (Tip: check if it's accessible in incognito mode). The recording should be under 8 minutes long.
  • Team Captain's Contact Number

Make sure all details are accurate to complete the submission process successfully.
